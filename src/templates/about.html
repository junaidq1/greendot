About greendotpeers.com

greendotpeers.com is a portal where consulting practitioners at Deloitte Consulting can search for and provide feedback on their colleagues (Managers, Senior Managers and PPDs) anonymously.

Management Consulting firms operate under a unique talent model. Junior practitioners are recruited into a talent pool with no direct managerial reporting heirarchy. The expectation from the firm is that they will own their own career and be responsible for utilization metrics. The way to do that is by getting to know senior practitioners and having them staff you on the enagegements they sell.

Yet, for a business model that is built on leveraging data for adding value, there is surprisingly little data available to junior practitioners on the prospective senior folks they can choose to work with.

Engagement teams are the one variable that tend to have the single greatest impact on the quality of a practitioner's project experiece. greendotpeers.com aims to equip practitioners with data needed to conduct diligence on prospective M's, SM's and PPDs so they can make more imformed staffing decisions.

The portal provides detailed ratings on Manager level practitioners and above including how professionally nice they are, how professionally competent they area in their domain of expertise and how much other practitioners learned and enjoyed working with them
